Launch Uptake Modeler - Readme

Interactive dashboard for forecasting product adoption curves, fitting to historical launch data, and comparing strategic scenarios.

What This Tool Does

  • Simulates cumulative and incremental adoption over time.
  • Compares up to 4 scenarios on chart, table, and KPI milestones.
  • Fits models to observed data and reports R2, RMSE, MAPE.
  • Exports data and supports shareable URL state.

Model Overview

1) Logistic

Symmetric S-curve. Best when acceleration/deceleration are balanced and inflection at 50% is reasonable.

Parameters: L ceiling, k steepness, t0 inflection timing.

2) Gompertz

Asymmetric S-curve with slower early adoption and inflection near 36.8% of ceiling.

Best for: high early inertia launches (pharma, enterprise, complex onboarding).

3) Richards

Generalized logistic master model. nu controls asymmetry; nu=1 equals Logistic.

Best for: uncertain curve shape and analog-data fitting.

4) Bass Diffusion

Mechanistic diffusion model using innovation p and imitation q.

Best for: network effects, social contagion, durable goods.

5) Linear Ramp

Straight ramp baseline with lag and cap. Conservative planning benchmark under capacity/process constraints.

Fitting Best Practices

  • Use cumulative adoption percentage as input: period,adoption%.
  • Include early and middle phases to identify shape and timing robustly.
  • Compare fit metrics, but also sanity-check business realism.

Scenario Planning Tips

  • Create downside/base/upside cases with different k, lag, and ceiling.
  • Use milestone deltas (10/50/90 and peak timing) to communicate timing risk.
  • Use volume mode and TAM for demand/supply planning.
